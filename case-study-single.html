CASE STUDY

Production Line Optimization In Adhesives Manufacturing

Altius Technologies Inc. has established itself as a leader in the technology industry, offering a wide range of services. With over 10+ years of expertise, we successfully served more than 200 clients.

Client overview

A global adhesives manufacturer struggling with inefficiencies in its production line due to machine downtime and raw material wastage.

Challenge

Enhance production efficiency and reduce wastage.

Solution Provided by Humeen:

  • Implemented a real-time production dashboard integrating data from IoT sensors on machines and ERP systems.
  • Key metrics tracked: OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness), material wastage, and batch processing times.
  • Added predictive maintenance alerts to minimize unplanned downtime.

Solution Provided by Humeen:

20%

Increased production efficiency

15%

Reduced raw material wastage, saving $2 million annually

Key Takeaways: Real-time data integration is crucial for modern manufacturing.
