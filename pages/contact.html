Common Questions

Things you might be wondering.

What kind of businesses do you usually work with?

We work with a mix of small businesses, growing brands, and early-stage companies. Some come to us with a clear plan, others come with just an idea. What matters more than size is whether the business is serious about building something long term.

I don’t have a clear plan yet — can you still help?

Yes. Many clients come to us feeling unsure about what they actually need. We start by understanding your business and goals, then guide you step by step. You don’t need to have everything figured out before talking to us.

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on the type of work. A website or app usually takes a few weeks, while marketing and content work is ongoing. After our first discussion, we’ll give you a clear and realistic timeline — no guesswork.

How do you know if something is actually working?

We focus on results that matter to your business, not just numbers on a screen. This could be enquiries, user activity, engagement, or performance improvements. We explain everything clearly so you understand what’s happening and why.

Will I be involved during the project?

Yes, but we keep it simple. We update you at key stages and take your feedback before moving forward. You won’t be overloaded with technical details, but you’ll always know what’s going on.

What happens after the project is completed?

We don’t disappear after delivery. If you need updates, improvements, or ongoing support, we’re available. Many of our clients continue working with us as their business grows.

What is a Leads Assistant and how does it help?

A Leads Assistant helps manage incoming enquiries so nothing gets missed. It can handle basic responses, organise leads, and make sure enquiries reach the right place. This helps improve response time and reduces manual follow-ups, while your team handles the actual conversations.

How do you communicate during a project?

We keep communication simple and clear. You’ll know what’s happening, what’s next, and when your input is needed. Updates are shared at key stages so you’re informed without being overwhelmed.

What do you need from us to get started?

To begin, we mainly need clarity on your business, goals, and expectations. Even if everything isn’t fully defined, an initial discussion is enough. From there, we help structure the next steps together.
